About

BiteBuilder is your go-to solution for effortless meal planning.

Our goal is to simplify your daily meal preparation by providing customized meal plans based on your caloric needs. Whether you're new to calorie counting or just looking for a convenient way to manage your diet, BiteBuilder is here to help.


Our Mission

BiteBuilder aims to make daily meal planning hassle-free by generating three balanced meals—breakfast, lunch, and dinner—based on your daily caloric limit. We strive to save you time and effort while helping you maintain a healthy diet.
